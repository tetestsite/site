About The Experience

Our Mission

The Experience exists to glorify God by helping people Connect, Grow, and find Purpose.

  • Connect — Building vibrant, loving relationships with God and people.
  • Growth — Becoming mature disciples of Jesus.
  • Purpose — Serving others and sharing our faith with passion.

Our True North

We serve people in Glendale and surrounding areas who want a fresh start. With no judgment, we point to a fulfilling life in Jesus.

Our Beliefs

  • There is one true God — Jesus Christ — Creator and Redeemer of all.
  • The Bible is God’s perfect, authoritative Word and our guide for life.
  • Jesus’ birth, life, death, and resurrection bring salvation and direct access to Him.
  • We practice water and Spirit baptism and communion, sharing the gospel in community.
