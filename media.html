Life Groups / Grupos de Amistad

Life is better together. Groups meet weekly across the city for community, prayer, and discipleship. / La vida es mejor juntos. Los grupos se reúnen cada semana para comunidad, oración y discipulado.

Why Groups?

In Acts 2:42–47 believers met in homes, shared meals, prayed, and cared for one another. We’re continuing that rhythm today.
